Privacy Policy

Your photos stay yours.

BlindBox is designed with a local-first approach. Your photo library is accessed only with your permission and processed on your device.

No Uploads Your photos are not uploaded to any server.
No Tracking BlindBox does not use advertising or tracking SDKs.
Local First Photo organization happens on your device.

Photos Access

BlindBox requests access to your photo library only so the app can display and help you organize photos selected through Apple's Photos permission system.

Photos are processed locally on your device. BlindBox does not upload, store, sell, or share your photos.

Data Collection

BlindBox does not collect personal data, analytics data, advertising identifiers, or tracking information.

The app does not require an account and does not operate a backend server for storing user data.

User Feedback

If you contact the developer by email, any information you choose to include in that email will be used only to respond to your message, investigate issues, or improve the app.

Third-Party Services

BlindBox does not use third-party advertising SDKs or analytics SDKs.

Children’s Privacy

BlindBox does not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated when the app changes. The latest version will always be available on this page.
