When reporting a bug

To help diagnose the issue faster, you may include:

  • Your iPhone model
  • Your iOS version
  • A short description of what happened
  • Optional screenshots or screen recordings

About BlindBox

BlindBox is an independent iPhone app for organizing photos through a random, card-based cleanup experience. It is designed to stay simple, private, and focused on your memories.
